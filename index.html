FAQ

Common Questions

Can't find your answer? Call us or chat on WhatsApp — we're always here.

📞 Call Us Now 💬 WhatsApp Chat
Do you provide home visit service?
▼
Yes! All our services are home-visit based. Our technician will come to your location at the scheduled time with all necessary tools and spare parts.
How quickly can a technician arrive?
▼
For standard bookings, we typically arrive within 2–4 hours. For emergency calls, we target arrival within the hour. We cover all areas across Salem district.
What brands do you service?
▼
We service all major brands including LG, Samsung, Voltas, Daikin, Whirlpool, Hitachi, Godrej, Blue Star, Haier, IFB, Bosch, Kent, and many more. If you don't see your brand listed, just call us.
Do you use genuine spare parts?
▼
Absolutely. We use only genuine, manufacturer-approved spare parts for all repairs. We never compromise on part quality, which is why our repairs last. All parts come with a 90-day warranty.
Is there a warranty on repairs?
▼
Yes. Every repair carried out by SusiElectronics comes with a 90-day service warranty. If the same problem recurs within this period, we'll fix it at no additional charge.
How does pricing work?
▼
We charge a nominal diagnostic visit fee. After diagnosis, we provide a full written estimate for your approval before any repair work begins. No hidden charges, no surprises.
Can I reach you at midnight for emergencies?
▼
Yes! We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including public holidays. Call our emergency number at any hour and a real person will answer to help you.
